Last Updated: January 2026

1. Acceptance

By registering with, training under, or participating in any program, tournament, trial, league, tour, or activity organized by NCL Elite Coaching & All India Club Cricket Association (hereafter referred to as “NCL Elite”, “the Association”, or “the Management”), all players, parents/guardians, team officials, and members fully agree to the following Terms & Conditions:

Squad Lists & Official Information Source

1. All official team squads, updates, announcements, fixtures, and notices will be published on the official website.
2. Players can view squads by going to the Navigation Bar → Teams → Select the desired team.
3. Only information published on official NCL Elite platforms shall be considered valid.
4. Verbal messages, unofficial posters, or third-party announcements will not be binding.

Memberships & Acceptance

1. All players must complete the official registration process and submit accurate personal, age, and cricketing.
2. Registration does not guarantee match selection. Selection is purely based on performance, discipline, fitness, and team.
3. By joining NCL Elite, the player agrees to follow all rules, codes, and decisions taken by the Association.
4. The Association reserves the right to accept or reject any registration without obligation to disclose reasons.

Team Allocation & Player Permanency (IMPORTANT)

1. Once a player is allotted to a club/team, that player will be considered a permanent member of that team for the entire 2026–27 session.
2. Transfers, releases, or team changes will only occur if officially announced or approved by the Association.
3. No player is allowed to represent any other NCL Elite team or rival association in overlapping tournaments without written permission.
4. Team loyalty and continuity are core principles of NCL Elite.

1. Official team kits (jerseys, trousers, caps, training wear where applicable) will be provided by the respective team / Association.
2. Wearing the official kit during matches, ceremonies, and media activities is compulsory.
3. Players must not modify kits, cover logos, or wear conflicting sponsorships without approval.
4. Damage or loss of issued equipment due to negligence may be chargeable.
